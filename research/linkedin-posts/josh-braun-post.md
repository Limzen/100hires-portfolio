## Content

"I’m not the right person." How do you respond?

RESPONSE 1: No empathy. Make it about you.
"Hi Sarah, No problem. Do you know who the right person is? Thanks, Josh"
WHAT THIS SIGNALS: Self-focused. Creates work. Adds pressure.

RESPONSE 2: Empathy. Understand them.
"Hi Sarah, Thanks for letting me know. I'm guessing you get plenty of emails that assume you're responsible for things you're nowhere near. That has to get old. I appreciate you taking a second to point it out instead of just deleting my email. No need to do any homework on my behalf. If the right person happens to come to mind, great. If not, I completely understand. Thanks again, and hope you have a great week. Josh"
WHAT THIS SIGNALS: Shows understanding. Removes obligation. Builds goodwill. Keeps the door open.

The goal isn't to get an answer today. It's to build relationships that create opportunities.
